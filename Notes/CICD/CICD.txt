ðŸ‘‰ Continuous Delivery (CD)
- Definition: Ensures that every code change is automatically built, tested, and prepared for release.
- Deployment: The software is always in a deployable state, but release to production may still require manual approval.
              It requires to clicking on button to deploy as it requires manual intervention.
- Goal: Shorten release cycles, reduce risk, and make deployments routine.
- Example:
    - After CI passes, artifacts are packaged and deployed to a staging environment.
    - Ops team can approve and push to production.
